ATS vs Wellness Score

ATS Scoring

Uses specific criteria such as keywords, formatting, and qualifications that match the job description. Resumes are filtered based on whether they meet these criteria, often resulting in a binary outcome (qualified or not qualified).

Wellness Score

Evaluates multiple aspects of a resume (content quality, formatting, keyword usage) and provides a score that reflects overall effectiveness, rather than simply filtering candidates.
